# Inventory-Sales-Performance-Analysis
A detailed visual analysis of inventory sales performance datasets to identify and predict sales, revenue, and employee attendance metrics using Power BI, Excel and SQL. This project helps analyze stock availability, product sales, and inventory trends through interactive visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The Inventory Sales Dashboard is designed to help businesses monitor inventory and sales in one place. It enables users to identify low-stock products, track revenue, and analyze sales across different product categories.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 📦 Total Inventory Stock Overview
- 💰 Total Sales Revenue KPI
- 📈 Product-wise Sales Analysis
- 📊 Stock Quantity Monitoring
- ⚠️ Reorder Level Tracking
- 🗂️ Category-based Filtering
- 🔍 Interactive Slicers for Data Exploration

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

The dashboard includes:

- **KPI Card**
  - Total Stock

- **Clustered Bar Chart**
  - Total Sales Revenue by Sales Date

- **Clustered Column Chart**
  - Sales Quantity by Product

- **Inventory Table**
  - Product Name
  - Stock Quantity
  - Reorder Level

- **Interactive Slicers**
  - Product Category
  - Stock Quantity

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains inventory and sales information including:

- Product Name
- Category
- Stock Quantity
- Reorder Level
- Sales Quantity
- Sales Revenue
- Sales Date

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Monitor current inventory levels.
- Track total sales revenue over time.
- Identify products with high sales volume.
- Detect products approaching reorder levels.
- Filter reports by category and stock quantity.
